About Us

You will experience the finest food, which perfectly combines the ancient traditions and authentic flavors of India. The secret of Indian Cuisine is in the proper use of selected spices to bring out the rich flavor, aroma, and character in food.By proper use of different techniques such as roasting or frying the spices whole, or grinding them to make a paste, it is possible to draw different flavors from the same spice.

Food Allergy Notice - Please be advised that food prepared here may contain these ingredients : milk, eggs, wheat, soybean, peanuts, tree nuts, fish and shellfish.
